## 👋 About Me

I am a **Blue Team practitioner** focused on defensive security — how defenders observe, detect, and reason about security events through logs, alerts, and network signals.

Most of my learning is **hands-on**. I design and build home lab environments to understand what security data actually looks like from a SOC analyst's perspective — not just how tools work, but **how analysts think**.

Currently in a cybersecurity bootcamp (funded by Agentur für Arbeit, Germany), finishing end of 2026. My goal is to grow as a **SOC Analyst** — with a strong focus on understanding the detection logic behind alerts, not just triaging them. I want to know *why* a rule fires, *what* it means in context, and *how* to make it better. That curiosity is what drives the Iron Watch lab series.

## 🧠 Lab Philosophy

> *"Document what is observed, not what is assumed."*

- Strong network isolation — every zone exists for a reason
- Controlled exposure — no unnecessary attack surface
- Understand the tool before trusting it
- A detection rule that is not tested is not a detection — it is a guess
